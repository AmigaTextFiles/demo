Manga film reviews




                             by Kei/Carnage


 Any  of  you  that  know  me  know  that  I'm  a manga/anime nut with a
collection  fast  approaching 300 titles.  Anyway, here's a quick review
of my fabourite titles.

                                -+- -+-

Title : Ghost In The Shell
Rating: 9/10

 A  cyberpunk  manga  based in the near future.  Large corporations rule
and several companies provide cyborg bodies to everyone and anyone.  The
main  character  is  a female cyborg attached to a special police force.
Her  partner  even  sports  some  special  cyborg lenses as described in
Gibsons Neuromancer!
 A  criminal  known  as  the  Puppet  Master  is erasing both humans and
cyborgs  memories  and replacing them with generated ones so they do not
know who they are only what he wants them to do.  The humans are given a
fake  memory  so  they  live normal lives and are hard to detect.  These
people are known as ghosts, hence ghosts in their shells.
 This  anime  style  is very modern and I look forward to seeing more of
it's  type.   While in London recently I noticed a store had a full size
model  cyborg  of  the  main character in naked form on display in their
window  with  bits  of  flesh  stripped  back  revealing  her servos and
mechanisms and wires protroduing from everywhere.

At present there is no cyberpunk manga that can beat this film.

                                -+- -+-

Title : Megazone 23
Rating: 8/10

 An  area  of land contained within an artificial environment to prevent
outside  contaminants  from  getting  in,  and containg large citties is
known as a megazone.  The story centres around a group of hackers turned
proffesionals  that  are  used  against  other  hackers  known  as  "net
jackers".   They  steal electricity and information by re-routing it and
can  gain  control to most of the cities equipment much to the annoyance
of  the  policing  body.   However  this police force is corrupt and the
newly  recruited  hackers  and  net  jackers  eventually  join forces to
release Eve in order to correct the problem by giving her control of the
central mainframe.

It  gets more complicated than this but to sum it up, it is a modern day
Akira  featuring  hackers  who  want  to  return power to the people and
restore our original way of living.

                                -+- -+-

Title : M.D.Geist
Rating: 7/10

 M.D  meaning  Most  Dangerous,  is  a  Geist.  Geist's are artificially
created  humans  used  in  war.  M.D.Geist however became so violent and
dangerous  that  he  was confined to a geostationary orbitting satellite
prison.   Suspended  in  cryogenics  for  years and forgotten about, his
satellite  accidentally  crashes back down to the plannets surface where
he  is  released.  Challenged by a gang of bandits he kills their leader
by cutting off his arms.  Now with the most powerfull gang behind him he
takes on his former creators and joins his former enemies but he becomes
insane.
 Further  on in the desert another Geist plays god to a colony of humans
protecting him from the human eating robots now plaguing the surrounding
cities.   M.D.Geist  wonders  into  camp and the ruling Geist challenges
him.
 This  is  where  I  started  loosing  the  thread  of the story because
M.D.Geist is portrayed as evil and the other Geist as good but M.D.Geist
is invincible.

                                -+- -+-

Title : Cyber City ODEO 808 File 1 : Virtual Death
Rating: 8/10

 The  sound  track  to  this is great!  Three criminals are sentenced to
life  sentences in prison but are offered a deal.  For every job they do
for  the  company  they  get time taken off their sentences.  However it
will  take  an  enormos amount of tasks to erase the sentences completly
and if they die in the process, the company looses nothing.
 There  are  3  files  to  this  series  each  based around one of the 3
characters.  The first one is my favourite as it is more cyberpunk.  The
second is about hacking and the third, vampyres.

Title : Gunhed
Rating: 7/10

 Listed  as  anime  but  actually a film.  Set in the future, scavengers
land  on  an  island  controlled  by a central computer.  They only want
parts  to  sell  on  the  black  market  but they end up dying for their
troubles.   One  of  the  scavengers  and a female (only american in the
film)  and  2  kids  are  all  that  is  left and try to escape using an
abondoned tank with an AI processor, known as Gunhed.

                                -+- -+-

Title : AD Police Episodes 1-3
Rating: 8/10

 I'm  a  big  fan  of  Bubblegum  Crisis and when I heard this was based
around this I had to check it out.  However it is based on the character
Leon in BGC and is set before BGC which is weird because in BGC, Leon is
a bit of a wanker but in this he's a tough bastard.
 It's  a  bit  like  Dominion  Tank  Police  in  style but it has a more
cyberpunk  edge  to  it  especially  if  you used to read the old 2000AD
comics  like  I  did.   BTW, does anyone know what will happen to 2000AD
comics in the year 2000 which is getting nearer?

Title : Angel Cop Parts 1-6
Rating: 7/10

 Angel is a cop with an attitude.  Taking no shit from no one and riding
motor  bikes  like  nobodies  business,  she  takes on anyone giving her
attitude  from terrorists using porshes to launch missiles to mad cyborg
bitches.

                                -+- -+-

Title : Armitage III Parts 1-4
Rating: 8/10

 Armitage  is  an AI cyborg.  Part of a series of cyborgs that are being
hunted  by  someone wishing their extinction.  Armitage is a cop however
and  is  teamed  with a human to catch this guy.  However Armitage falls
for her human partner but cannot come to terms with who she is.

                                -+- -+-

Title : Battle Angel Alita
Rating: 8/10

 Alita  is  a cyborg that falls in love with Hugo, a black marketeer and
wanted  criminal sentenced to death.  Alita makes her living bringing in
people like Hugo and collecting the bounties on their heads (literarly).
However  she cannot bring herself to bring in Hugo and when other bounty
hunters hunt Hugo she illiminates them.

Title : Catgirl Nuku-Nuku Episodes 1-3
Rating: 7/10

 A young boys father escapes from the company he works for with the body
shell  of  his new prototype cyborg.  The boys cat dies and he is sad so
the father implants the cats brain into the cyborgs body shell.  The boy
then  has a playmate in the form of a beautiful female girl (the cyborg)
but  with  a  mind  of  a  playfull  kitten.  It's a comedy based around
Nuku-Nuku  who  wants  to play all the time but does not realise her own
strength  (she  can pick up cars to get at a ball that rolled underneath
it).

Title : Dominion Tank Police Acts 1-10
Rating: 8/10

 A  young female police officer and her partner build a prototype police
tank  that  is  smaller than the rest but faster, more agile and tougher
(called  Napolean).   They patrol the streets hunting criminals in their
tanks.   However 2 cyborg (yes cyborg, ok so I have a lot of films about
cyborgs..)  cat  girls (puma sisters) are the tank polices main openents
as they carry out criminal acts with the uttermost of stupidity.

Title : Goku Midnight Eye 1-2
Rating: 7/10

 Goku  is sent on a mission and fails.  Left for dead, an unknown person
finds  him  and  implants  a  cybernetic eye into his head replacing his
damaged one.  Combined with a new weapon in the form of a cydrinical bar
which  extends  at  either  end  into an indistructable staff, Goku sees
things  he never saw before.  He returns to complete his mission with an
advantage that his openents were not expecting.

Title : Maison Ikkoku Parts 1-2
Rating: 7/10

 A  young  college student finds it hard to study in his flat because of
his  flat mates constantly holding parties in his room.  His studying is
made  harder  by  the  new  landlord  who  is a beautiful young woman he
developes the hots for.

Title : Moldiver Tapes 1-3
Rating: 7/10

 Moldiver is a project.  A special project designed by a hacker which is
held  in  a box that looks suspiciously like a walkman.  When held above
the   head  and  the  word  "Metamorforce"  is  spoken,  the  holder  is
transformed  into  Moldiver.  Designed for the project creator, Moldiver
is  a male superman clone but his young sister keeps stealing the device
and  transforms into a cute Moldiver which she uses to help get a guy to
notice  her.  However there is a criminal organisation trying to destroy
Moldiver  but  the  girl sees them as a mere irratation that gets in her
way so she swats them like flies much to their annoyance.

                                -+- -+-

Title : Ninja Scroll
Rating: 6/10

 A very blood thirsty adult movie this.  A young ninja feared throughout
the  land  is  tricked into helping an old man.  Teamed with a beautiful
girl  whose skin is pure poison to anyone that touches or kisses her, he
must complete his mission in order to live.

Title : Ranma (ANY)
Rating: 7/10

 There  are so many Ranma titles out there.  It's popular in Japan.  The
basic  storyline is a father and son are cursed by an old hag.  Whenever
the  father gets wet he turns into a giant panda.  Whenever the son gets
wet he turns into a beautiful girl.  This causes problems for the son as
when  he takes a shower and his mates walk in they see a beautiful girl.
Ranma  however  has  several loves both tring to obtain the phoenix pill
for him which will remove the curse.

Title : Riding Bean
Rating: 7/10

 Bean  and team are for hire to anyone requiring their services.  Framed
for  a  crime  they  did  not commit they must clear their names.  I had
never  seen  this  title  before but from what I can gather, Bean is not
human seen as how bullets don't seem to hurt him.  Just a guess.

                                -+- -+-

Title : Silent Mobius
Rating: 7/10

 A very weird set of films based around the concept that ESP exists.  In
the  future  a  cop department known as AMD recruits girls with high ESP
levels  to  help  protect the world from the demon dimension.  It's very
cutesy and romantic at times and you can't help but get the feeling that
it  was  created by people with no life and girlfriends but it's graphic
style  outways any fears of it being too crap.  Plus the storyline is so
entwined  and  deep  that you'd probably spend most of your time working
out who is who.

Title : Tenchi Muyo
Rating: 9/10

 One  of  my  favourite comedy mangas.  There are 2 storylines to Tenchi
depending  on  whether  you are watching the films or series.  The films
are  available  in  the  UK  as  tapes with 2 titles on each and in them
Tenchi  accidentally  frees  a demon who falls in love with him.  In the
series however he does not free the demon, she comes to see him.
 Tenchi  is hilarious and a most for any manga enthusiast.  Tenchi is in
the middle of a love triangle where the girls want his body and he wants
out.   The  cute  graphics and humour will make you laugh unless you get
the subtitled versions, read fast and you should still laugh though!

                                -+- -+-

Title : Uresei Yatsura (aka: Lum)
Rating: 6/10

 The  main  character is girl crazy except when it comes to Lum.  Lum is
crazy  about  settling  down  with  her  "darling"  but he's crazy about
anything  that  goes  by in a skirt.  This is a weird long winded comedy
with   weird  characters.   For  instance  the  postman  looks  like  an
over-weight penguin on a push bike!

                                -+- -+-

Title : Ushio & Tora 1-2
Rating: 6/10

 Ushio  frees  an  ancient  demon from his cellar called Tora.  With the
constant  threat  of  Ushio  placing him back in his former prison, Tora
reluctantly agrees to help Ushio battle evil demons.

                                -+- -+-

Title : Video Girl AI 1-6
Rating: 7/10

 A  lonely  heart  broken kid goes to rent a video from his local store.
The owner takes pity on him and gives him a special tape which plays for
6  days.   During  this time the video girl (her name is "Ai" pronounced
"eye")  will  teach  him  how  to win the love of his life.  However his
video  recorder is broken and Ai starts to fall in love with the boy but
she  knows  she  must  complete  her task soon and put her own feeling's
aside before her time is up.
