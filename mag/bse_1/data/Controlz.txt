Confused  by  the  complex  control  methods of this magazine?  NO?  Well, read
these  instructions  anyway.   (Especially, seeing as I could be arsed to write
them!):

@5
   ICON BAR:
   ~~~~~~~~~@1
   See that bar-shaped thing at the bottom of the screen with lots of
   icon-type things on it? That's you...err..that's the icon bar.

@3       INDEX           -@1 Clicking this will return you to the index pages 
                         after reading an article.

@3       MUZIK ON/OFF    -@1 Unless you're deaf this is pretty self-explanatory.

@3       FIRST PAGE      -@1 Moves you to the first page of an article or the
                         index.

@3       LAST PAGE       -@1 Moves you to the last page of an article or the
                         index.

@3       GO TO PAGE      -@1 Allows you to move to a specific page of an
                         article/index by typing page number followed by
                         Return.

@3       EXIT            -@1 A double-click on this will banish Beyond Sanit-E
                         from your screen forever.

@3       ARROWS          -@1 The up and down arrows will take up to the first
                         and last pages of an article/index respectively.
                         The left and right arrows will take you to the
                         previous or next pages of an article/index.

@5
    KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS:
    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~@1
    Here are some shortcut keys for the rat-haters.

@3       ESC             -@1 Return to index from article.

@3       SHIFT + ESC     -@1 Quit program.

@3       SPACE BAR       -@1 Go to a specific page of article/index.

@3       S               -@1 Toggle muzik on or off.

@3       CURSOR KEYS     -@1 Perform same functions as the arrow icons.
