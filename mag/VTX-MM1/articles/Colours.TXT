Colour Codes




Here are the colour codes to use for your Moon Monkey articles (if you
want!) To use a colour, put the line "!code=xxxyyy" on the line above
where you want to start using the colour.

xxx is the text colour
yyy is the background colour

Examples:

 !code=015129
!code=015129
Would Produce this colour scheme
