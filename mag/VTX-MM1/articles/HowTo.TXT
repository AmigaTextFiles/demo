HOW TO USE MOON MONKEY






Moon Monkey can be used in two different ways - Either with the keyboard
or mouse. (We cater for all tastes etc...)

KEYBOARD CONTROL ON INDEX PAGES
-------------------------------

CURSOR UP     - Move up an article
CUSSOR DOWN   - Move down an article
CURSOR RIGHT  - Move to the next index page
CURSOR LEFT   - Move to the previous index page

RETURN        - Read highlighted article
ESCAPE        - Quit Brainstorm

F1            - Turn music off
F2            - Turn music on




KEYBOARD CONTROL ON ARTICLE PAGES
---------------------------------

RETURN/CURSOR DOWN  - Read next page
ESCAPE              - Back to index

MOUSE CONTROL ON INDEX PAGES
----------------------------

Click On Up Arrow Icon     - Move up and article
Click On Down Arrow Icon   - Move down an article
Click On Right Arrow Icon  - Move to next index page
Click On Left Arrow Icon   - Move to the previous index page

Click On Circle Arrow Icon - Read highlighted article

Click On Article Name      - Read article

Click On Quit Icon         - Quit Brainstorm
