Type : util/pack
Version : 1.3
Requirements: 68020 or greater processor, WB 3.0 , AGA.
===================================================

De-Archiveur  is  an archiving intuition  interface to crunch and 
decrunch  file(s) with  archivers such as LHA, LZH, LZX, ZIP, DMS,
UNARJ, UNRAR. De-Archiveur can also examine an archive that 
mean you can extract, add or delete one file or more, read, play, 
listen one or more file(s) with your prefered programs. 
De-Archiveur can convert into 3 modes (LHA, LZX or ZIP) one or 
more archive(s) LHA, LZH, LZX, ZIP, UNARJ, UNRAR. 
LHA,LZH,LZX,ZIP,DMS,UNARJ,UNRAR are neccessary  for  using  
this program. De-Archiveur is a Blitz Basic non limited program.
