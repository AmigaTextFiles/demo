Rammstein : Herzeleid





I know this album has been released months ago, but I have discovered Rammstein recently at the Pukkelpop 97. I know their second album is out, but I have not yet bought it. Then STOP to criticize me and put an ear on this wonderful piece of plastic.

This album is a fist in your face. The power of the music combined with the ultra-deep voice of the singer ( who sings in german if you don't know it already ) produces a really aggressive sound. They also use samples here and there, and this adds a cyber touch to the ensemble.
The entire album is very powerfull and even the single "slower" song ( Seemann ) is strong in emotions and is violent sometimes.

I can't tell anymore than HEAR THIS CD NOW !!!. If you don't, you are missing something.
It's simple : here, this album is running in loop...

My quote : an enormous 10/10 !!! It's a total success.
