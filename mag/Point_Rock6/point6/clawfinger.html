Clawfinger : Clawfinger





The previous album of the band was not especially good because it was the exact copy of the powerfull Deaf Dumb Blind.
So, what is the situation now ???

Clawfinger is still playing in RAP-METAL style, but the band has quite evoluted since the last album. Their music is more diversified now : they use again samples everywhere with hard guitars but they have added an undefined little something more and this is exactly this undefined little something which does the job.

Clawfinger the album is really interesting. Of course, there are some songs in the direct line from Nigger and The Truth ( like Biggest & The Best ), but some songs differs from others ( Hold your Head Up, Chances,... ).

Well, my quote for this album : I can decide myself between 7 or 8, so I put an decent 7.5 /10
