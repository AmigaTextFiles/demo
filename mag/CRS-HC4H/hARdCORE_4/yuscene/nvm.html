Once, there was a man called Sule (which is short for Suleiman the magnificent, but that's not how he got his handle). He had (and still have) an Amiga, liked it, worked on it. One day, he got bored of sitting and working just for himself. And that's how it all started...

He formed a BBS, called Universe, and so become the ruler of universe;) BBS was (and still is) WereWolf Technologies HQ. Sule has released a few ASCII collections under WWT name. However... WWT wasn't too active. So he decided to form his own crew.

He gathered a few men, and after a while - new group was born! The name is nEVERmIND, and the historical date is 4th April 1996. Group started work with 5 members: bEAt lORd, DNK, Rudy, Sule and Zidar (Yugoslav guys, don't smile, it's his last name!).Later he changed it 2 mR.W!. Later, sPEY b0NDEN joined.
