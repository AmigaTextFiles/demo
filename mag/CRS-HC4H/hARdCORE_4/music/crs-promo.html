we are proud to announce that this is our first release completelly digitally mastered and recorded on compact disc. what you are holding in your hands is the copy of our master cd with the same material on it.

why hARdCoRRoSioN and not some 'nice'n'spacey' name ?

we spent many hours thinking and rethinking about name(s) for our separate projects and finally agreed that we should stick under one ideology and not to split up between some fictious project names. in other words it means that DOMINATOR, ALEX and PARANOID, together with their projects BRAIN SYSTEM BIOWARE, DISASTER AREA and MIND ABUSE are going to appear in future releases under one name: hARdCoRRoSioN.

what the fuck hARdCoRRoSioN stands for, you might wonder ?

we think HARDCORE means far more than just a music type. for us, HARDCORE is the way of life, way of thinking, way of expressing ourselves. CoRRoSioN is HARDCORE, and we are - hARdCoRRoSioN!
