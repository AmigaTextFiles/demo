DAMAGED - AGAiN
And again... hardcore... necessary for most of us... in large ammounts... and missing... bloody missing...



After coming home the morning after Scene Strike, I fell unconcious and although planning to go to Cinema Rex at noon or so, I jumped from bed at 4PM, pretty freaked out and stoned... Got my clothes on and jumped on a tram... vrm, vrm, vrrrrrm, up to Rex, where I came in AFTER all the heavy duty work was done. Hehehehe... Evil, aren't I?

We planned for the concert to begin at 6PM, but there was bloody NOONE there at that time... the problem was, there was bloody noone there at any time after that... so we just wasted a heck of a lot of time waiting for someone to show up, and fucked around with the equipment in the meantime. There was lots of heavy duty stuff there, power amps, a bunch of 'puters, alex's guitar, video beam bullshit, strobes, and all kinds of other hi tek mumbo-jumbo. It was one of the funnest "waitings" in my life.

Lots of modules were played, although the one that stuck in my head for quite a while after was Six Million Ways to Die, by RaatoMestari of the Mighty Jormas... Dominator just kept playing it, over and over and over and over... somehow, I got the impression that he liked that tune. Wonder where that came from?

So, at a certain point in time, someone finaly decided that we should go for it, no matter how small the audience was. That turned out to be one of the greatest ideas any one of us had in a while.

The last concert I visited was the one in Industry, where Domin8r was on stage as part of a g*a night, so I remember his last show to be a dehydrated hop-on-one-leg freaky bouncing-off-other-people overcrowded gig, which kicked serious ass, but the hoparound factor was quite limited. This time, everything was different.

As I judge it, there were around 50 people there, maybe slightly less, which is quite a success considering that Kalichanin was making a mega-ultra-turbo-g*a party the same evening, and also considering the fact that our gig was starting at 6PM... who on Earth goes out at that time, right? Again, I must say, it turned out to be an excellent idea...

So, at around eight o'clock (maybe slightly earlier) someone decided to commence the blastoff. First in line was a friend of Alex's, who played a breakbeatish tune of his, quite slow, but well done, nice for a warmup... Then it was supposed to be my turn, but I was cheated out of murdering the audience with my desintegrator sounds, so we skipped to the next ones in line, so...

Right after that, hARdCoRRoSioN was up there... bloody motherfuckin' DESTRUCTION. Kick ass. Major Ass. Fanplastic show. I am utterly speachless. We were bouncing around C-Rex all over, jumping, whaling, waving, raving... okay, not raving... but you get the idea. Almost the entire CoRRoSioN posse was down with the sound... Several other hardcore people in the room... and a few obvious misfits, who believed this to be some sort of "psychodelic trance" bullshit thing like the stuff they usually attend. They looked like idiots trying to do their classic "dance, party people!" type of faggot moves. We didn't pay much attention to them.

Kole Kojot & Ptica Trkacica Strikes Again Ipak (phew) were OKay, but, as sadly as it is, I must notice that they are getting quite monotoneous lately. I got in a bit of a fight with an Integra DJ over whether they were run over by time or not (or was he from Kozmik? Whatever...) and even though I defended them heavily back then, hearing them again and comparing them to hARdCoRRoSioN, I must revoke my harsh words I directed at whoever it was on the "Insomnia" list and admit - they have been run over. Heavily. They have kickass equipment, but they are obviously not using it right. I sat in front of the video beam watching Grommit during most of their performance. To be frank, I just waited for them to get it over with, so that I can take a leak. Fuck you if you didn't get the joke.

Then, for the grand finale, Dominator went up for his solo performance, aided only by Paranoid by the other Amiga, for the quality mixing. Do I realy have to describe the sort of mass murder he committed?

It felt like three hops from side to side, in the enormous C-Rex hall, while jumpin to his beats. Played some old stuff, some new stuff, some ancient stuff which, opposite to KKPTSAI's music, still is very up to date... Everybody was hopping around, Alex was up for doing a bit of the Pinball routine, which we did, bouncing around, and bothering other people. Infusion and Devlink kept getting in the way, and I whammed into Bepa twice, but other than that, C-Rex was empty enough for us to let all our surplus energy out. Not to mention Becha, who l8r described Domin8r's set as "busting us apart".

Then, the sad ending. C-Rex is a residential building, so we had to finish up the beats by 22:00 sharp. So we, or rather, he did, while we still hoped for a bit of on-core. Nope, none of that came, so we had to break it up...

The few fluorescent idiots went out to ride down to the Shlep, where they could try to get back to their usual dumb selves by listening to some crap g*a... The non-corrosive people who were around greeted the posse and took off... While we remained to scrape the remains of the equimpent off the stage, pack it up, and go home.

To cut the "aftershock" crap short, after playing some of Djordje Balashevic's greatest hits on Alex's guitar, we packed everything, and fled the place. M-G took me and my Motherfucker home in his vehicle, and exhausted, but fucked-up-happy, I installed the MF back in it's place, and dropped back to bed.

No ringing in the morning this time, unfortunately, so I can't finish this article off as cool as I did with the last one... but what the fuck, hardcore still does continue...

... and it will never fuckin' die!!!

Phuzzy Logik of CoRRoSioN
