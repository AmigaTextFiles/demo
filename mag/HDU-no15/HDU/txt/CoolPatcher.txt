Introduction
With the help of dA cOOL pATCHER (DCP) you can patch your 
favourite games/programs for your personal needings. For 
example remove nervy copy protections, set unlimited lifes or 
weapons and alot more. DCP comes with over 150 predefined 
patches which you can apply to your games easily. You can also 
unpatch patched games. DCP is able to patch files and disks. So 
'NDOS' games are no problem for DCP!
