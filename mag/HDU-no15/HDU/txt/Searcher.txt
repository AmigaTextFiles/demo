=========================================
Short : The Ultimate File Finder
Author: pierre.rivasseau@esial.u-nancy.fr (Pierre RIVASSEAU)
Requirements: OS2.0+, reqtools.library, locale.library
Distribution: shareware
=========================================

Searcher is a file finder with many options. Some features :
Workbench interface.
Full tooltypes and CLI arguments support.
Search by size, date or type (IFF picture, executable,...).
Case sensitive.
Automatic stop.
Display buffer.
Locale.library support.
Click & Play with finded files (show a picture, ...).
Search with index.
Management of index.
And MANY others...
