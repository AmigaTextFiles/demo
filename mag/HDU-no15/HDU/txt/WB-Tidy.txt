=========================================
Short : Tidy up your Workbench windows
Author : simon@gadge.u-net.com (Simon Hitchen)
=========================================

A little program that lets you diddle around with the size and 
position of your Workbench windows.

* Controlled via it's own GUI
* Four modes - Cascade, Stack, Tile, Child
* Grab & Undo functions
* Makes no permanent changes
* Comprehensive guide
