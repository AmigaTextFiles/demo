============================================
OktaPussy - New Driver set for Oktagon, etc.
Author : olli@elaborate-bytes.com (Oliver Kastl)
Requires: OS 2.04
Date:     14.06.98
============================================

Features:
- New drivers for ALF-III, Oktagon 508/2008/4008, TandemCD,
AlfaPower, AT-Bus 2008
- Installs in the RDSK Blocks of your Harddisk - super fast boot!
- 4 methods of installation : RDSK-Blocks, Reset-Resident(with 
LoadIDE), Expansion driver, Direct-Start.
- All devices support the TD64 command set. Drives >4GB 
shouldn't be a problem any more!

A new release of the ancient HDInstTools program can be found 
in a different archive (hdinst.lha).
