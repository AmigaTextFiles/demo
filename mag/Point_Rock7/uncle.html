Uncle Meat : Underneath





This is the album of a friend. Heheheh Afreak, don't be afraid, you know already that I like Underneath so I won't throw it in flames ;-)

Although it's the second album of the belgian band Uncle Meat, they like to say that it's their first real album. Previous one ( Swallow ) was a kind a very good demo. They have in fact rerecorded 2 songs from Swallow on the next album.

For this CD, the band has been produced by Collin Richardson ( who wanted to produce them... It's surely a sign ! ). You know, it's the guy responsible of the mighty sound of Fear Factory, Machine Head... It's not a beginner !

But the music ??? Yes the music, let's speak about it... The band says they have lot's of influences from bands of 80's : Sisters of Mercy, Depeche Mode... Quite diversified influences in fact. The result is a powerfull music with use of samples, not purely metal.

Best songs of the album are ( for me ) :Turn Away, Underneath, 22 ( Very slow but very dark ) and Tangled . But as I like always what others don't like, make yourself your own opinion.

My quote : 7.5/10
