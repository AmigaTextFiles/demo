Heads up !!!

Before anything, I would like to wish you all the best for 1998. I hope this year will give you plenty of things.
In musical area, we could expect great things in 1998 : I have heard that maybe a festival would be held in Germany and featuring Judas Priest and Iron Maiden, we could also expect a new tour of Metallica ( why not ? ). I have also read that Maiden wants to come back to the shows of their glorious years. After all, they are not the firsts to do that ( i.e. Kiss ).

As long as I have support ( and I have it believe me ), Point & Rock will continue to see the light of day. I really love that work and I would not like to stop it now... I just want to apologise if some delays appears in the publication of the mag. I have so many pages to maintain updated : the mag, my homepage, a site of norvegian friends...

In this issue, I have managed to interview or get some stuff from 2 bands : I have net-interviewed the drummer of Uncle Meat, a member of Syrens Call has mailed me an article about his band.
As always : news from everywhere, your charts, lyrics (Reload from Metallica)...

Enjoy your visit on this site, come as often as you want. My work is finished... until next edition ;-)
