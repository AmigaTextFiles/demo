Rammstein : Sehnsucht





This is the second album of Rammstein. The first one has been tested in P&R 6 and had a final quote of 10/10. Sehnsucht is also extremely powerfull, but is a little different from Herzeleid.

It seems the band has decided to be more intelligent in their compositions. Herzeleid was the equivalent of a fist in your face, this one is more diversified. It begins with an arab sample, followed soon by a martial drum. If you go some tracks further, you can discover some pure diamonds : "Klavier", "Spiel Mit Mir" where the voice of Till Lindemann is more calm than on other titles. There is a lot of emotions in his voice.
"Klavier" is also the slowest song of the album.

As in the previous album, the keyboards are fully used with tons of samples. It may sound strange sometimes ( "Du Hast" begins like a techno song ), but it's really effective.
All the lyrics are in german, but they are printed also in english, to discover what they say !!!

This album is excellent and deserves yet again the 10/10.
