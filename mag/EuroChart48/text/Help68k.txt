Use the arrow buttons to navigate. Arrow left and right scrolls the 
article one page to the left and right respectively. Arrow up loads 
the article above the one you are currently reading, while arrow down 
loads the article that is below the one you are currently reading.«»

«»

«»

can select the different music pieces (or turn off the music if you 
so prefer.«»
«»
