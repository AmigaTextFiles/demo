HOW TO USE THE EC C64«»
«»
«c3»By Xeron of IRIS«»
«»«as»«c2»
You will need:«»
«»
A Commodore 64 microcomputer system, or compatible«»
A Commodore 1541, 1541-II or compatible disk drive«»
A monitor or television, preferably colour«»
A cup of tea«»
A packet of biscuits«»
«»
Method:«»
«»
1. Insert EC, disk 1 side 1, into your Commodore 1541, 1541-II or compatible
   disk drive unit.«»
«»
2. Nibble a biscuit«»
«»
3. Power up your Commodore 64 microcomputer system using the switch on the
   right hand side.«»
«»
4. Take a sip of the cup of tea«»
«»
5. At the BASIC prompt, type LOAD "*",8,1«»
«»
6. Drink the tea, eat some biscuits«»
«»
7. After a while you should be presented with the Eurochart diskmag
   index.«»
«»
8. Enjoy the diskmag!«»
«»
Hints and tips:«»
«»
One of the lines of text should be flashing. This is the article indicator.
You can move the article indicator by pressing the "/\ CURS \/" key. Doing
so with shift held moves it the other way. Once the article you wish to read
is highlighted, press the RETURN key to load it up.«»
«»
You can go forward and back a page at a time using the "<= CURS =>" key.
Pressing it on its own will go forward a page. Pressing it with shift will
go back a page.«»
«»
At any time press I to return to the index. F1 brings up a help page.«»
«»
In the articles, the "/\ CURS \/" key can be used to go to the next or
previous article.«»
«»
At times, EC C64 will prompt you to insert other disks and press a key. In this
instance, insert the correct disk the right way up and press any key!«»
«»
If you enjoy the EC C64 experience, why not tell your friends?«»
