High Anxiety:
-------------

High Anxiety was presented at the Assembly '95 in Helsinki/Finland.

This demo should run on all configurations with AGA and 2 MB chipmem.
Maybe some problems appear if you start it from a MC68040 processor.

If this demo doesn't run from your CD-Rom correctly, then copy
it on your Hard-Drive.
