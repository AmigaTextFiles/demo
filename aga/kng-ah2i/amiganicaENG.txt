Once again we bring you one of the best parties of the lasy year!

Ismét megrendezzük a tavalyi év egyik legjobb scene party-jának
kikiáltott 'nica-t! 

The place is the same, but this time we 'upgraded' it a bit to get
more space for more machines.

A hely ugyanaz, bár egy kicsit 'megnagyobbítjuk', azért hogy még több 
asztal, és így még több gép elférjen.

Number of the organizers will be far biger, so we hope that everything
will work better... (there will be 2 organizer for each compo!)

A szervezõk létszáma a tavalyihoz képest jelentõsen megnõtt, így 
remélhetõleg sokkal zökkenõmentesebben fognak menni az események... 
(Minden egyes compo-ra 2-2 szervezõ embert állítunk rá!)

Again you have to pre-order your ticket, everyone gets a named-ticket,
which proves that you're not faking.
We'll check everyone's ticket at the entrance this time !
Good news that this year there'll be 400 tickets forsale, so we hope 
it'll be enough... Ofcoz it's recommended to order your ticket ASAP.

The ticket price is a bit higher than last year, but this money will
be used for setting up a little INTERnetwork of atleast 8-10 machines.
Anyway, if this won't work we'll give the money for the compo winners.
 
The only rule about the party is the same as last year! You can't bring
in any PC! (PC-like objects are dangerous too! :)

Last year there was no steeling, or bagging (as we know these are 
well-known party accessories). God saves the guy who we catch on 
something nasty thing!

The buffet will be up again with refreshments. Sadly we can't offer any
sleeping place, but we guess that anyone can stay up 24hs...

There'll be a nice big projector, and ofcoz the Crimson Jihad's music
equipment (known as telephonebox(a big loud speaker) and the paper bag
(a not-so-big, but loud amplifier) will guarantee the good vibes!
