REQUIEREMENT:
-PowerPC processor (604e is best, demo run hires!) or Pegasos.
-WarpOs or WOSemu (MorphOs)
-320*240*16 screenmode in CGXMODE
-AHI
-mpega.library
-mpega 3.5 (And 3.5 only for best performance! included.)

MUSIC:
-You have to configure the modeid used by MPEGA. The archive allready contain a .bat for Paula, Pegasos, and Repulse. If you have another sound card, just choose a modeid and set it with -I0x in a .bat (have a look at our exemples)
-If you have a delfina DSP, you can also remove MPEGA of the script and use DelfMPEG.
