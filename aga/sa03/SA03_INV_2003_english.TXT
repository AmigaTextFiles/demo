Syndeecate Apocalypse 2003 Invitation:
-------------------------------------

Syndeecate Apocalypse 2003 is an unique multiplatform 
computer party. SA03 is held for the third time in Rožnov
pod Radhoštìm town. It is for creative people, who are doing
in their free time multimedia work of art and they want to
show them to the public. But is it also for the newcomers,
who are interested in the "scene" and want to watch work of
other's people. 

Officially, we have choosed these computer platforms (in
alphabetical order): Amiga, Apple Macintosh, PC, ZX
Spectrum. If you are more interested in 8Bit scene you
should visit Forever in Slovak Republic/Trenèín instead. ZX
Spectrum on SA03 is only to show, that width of data-bus and
RAM size don't affect product quality.

ZX Spectrum:
+++++++++++


There will be 2 computers on the party place so you can choose
which one will be used for your production.

a) Didaktik Gama 128 with D80 disk drive, TAPE, AY3-8912 in ACB
stereo, RS232 and 3 channels 8Bit D/A.
This is Slovak clone of Sinclair ZX Spectrum 128 - black.

b) Sinclair ZX Spectrum +2A with external +3 Interface, 3,5" and
5,25" DD disk drive. AY is in ACB stereo. 

Music compo:
-----------
you can make both BEEP music and AY music. No matter WHICH Tracker
you will use. But if you use something special, PLAY routine MUST
BE included. Please write short read.me HOW to play your special
tune. Thanks to 3 channels 8Bit D/A you can use 8Bit samples in
Sample Tracker (Luxus Software).

- two tunes per musician
- 4 minutes per song
- you must specify the origin of music (cover/your own)

Graphics compo:
--------------
you can use classic 256x192 picture or Multicolor scr., interlace 256x384 or
multichrome picture. For Interlace, Multicolor and Multichrome
player routine MUST be included.

- two pictures per graphician

Demo compo:
----------
there is only system "limitations"

- special TAPE loaders are prohibited
- you must use Basic and LOAD "" command
- machine code must be loaded from 25000 and upper address
- you can't use command OUT 32765,XX for paging in Basic;
  everething must be done in machine code.
- +3 system friendly = 17th and 23rd page must be
  loaded&decrunched at last, after this, ei instruction with
  basic's calls is very dangerous for +3 system. To be absolutely
  sure, delete +3 paging routines at 23296.
- when you are not familiar with +3 or there is no chance to try
  your production under +3 don't worry; we will run your demo on
  DG128 or I will try to +3 fix it :)

Intro compo:
-----------
- only length limitations : 512B, 1024B or 4096B.

General rules:
-------------
All  contributions  must  have form.txt included and filled.
Length  of  files,  which  are  not  used  for  running  the
demo/play  the  music/show the picture is NOT included.  Pay
attention  to  the  time  length of your production.  Longer
ones  will be stopped prematurely.  The caregory with a very
few  amount of contribuions may be moved to WILD compo.  You
can  NOT  compete  with  already  presented  contribution or
contributions  presented  BEFORE Syndeecate.  If the name of
contribution is too long, we can cut it.

 General rules for all PC demos and intros:

maximal screen resolution: 800x600
you can use 3D acceleration
must be possible to interrup by pressing Esc key
must be able to run WITHOUT sound card
must NOT be interactive
must NOT confuse operating system
must free all the memory and system resources at the end

 PC demo

max.size: 8MB (8388608 bytes)
max.length: 6 minutes
you can use Flash and Java also


 PC 64KB intro

max.size: 64KB (65536 bytes)
max.length: 5 minutes


 PC 4KB intro

max.sizet: 4KB (4096 bytes)
max.length: 2 minutes
can NOT read from FDD/HDD


 PC 256B intro

max.size: 256 bytes
max.length: 30 seconds
can NOT read from FDD/HDD


 General rules for MUSIC and MODULE:

max.length: 4 minutes
must be your own composition
one song per musician


 MUSIC

max.sizet: 6MB (6291456 bytes)
accepted formats: all types


 MODULE

max.sizet: 800KB (819200 bytes)
accepted formats: IT, XM, S3M, MOD, MDL


 PIXELED PICTURE

max.resolutioní: 640x480
accepted formats: PNG, GIF, TGA, etc.

one screen per graphician


 RAYTRACED PICTURE

max.resolution: 640x480
accepted formats: PNG, GIF, TGA, atd.

one screen per graphician


 PICTURE
