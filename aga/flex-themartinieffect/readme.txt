Loaderror on the keys:
Here is your c2p style demo where we've 
gone an extra mile of interleaving c2p 
with effects in places. There is a new 
3d engine doing what these engines tend 
to do as well. Antialiasing, glow,
normalmaps, compiled particles being
some of the interesting features.

Special thanks to the Vampire devs for 
helping us debug the sound routine so it 
works on the next gen Amigas!
