 Technical information:
 ----------------------

_The Texture Mapping Routine use 1 Instruction/Pixel. 
 9 muls / polygon, no divs

_The True Map is corriged every 8 pixels.
 2 divs are used to corrige U, V texture coords.

_Bump Routine use 2 add, 1 sub every pixel.

_Tore Object is constitued of 400 faces.

_All sources was merged into one, no loader are used.
